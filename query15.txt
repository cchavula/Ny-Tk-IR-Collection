<title> kupima wodwala

<desc> Description: 
Identify ways and instruments for examining a sick person

A relevant document should have ways and instruments for examining a  sick person. This can be what questions to ask a sick person, what instruments can be used to examine a sick person and what procedures are used to examine a sick person.
